Why soft and hard cheese?

We recommend you track soft and hard cheese separately because they tend to cause migraines differently in different people.

Soft cheeses include brie, cream cheese and bleu cheese. Hard cheeses are firm - like cheddar and gouda.

If you want to track a specific cheese (e.g. cheddar cheese), you will have an opportunity later to set up custom triggers.
